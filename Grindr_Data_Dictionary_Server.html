Data Dictionary

Thank you for submitting your request to us. At Grindr, your privacy is important to us and we are proud to support your ability to access and understand the data we collect about you.

The information you will receive through our Download My Data tool is largely already available to you through the app and may vary depending on the way you have used Grindr. In general, this information includes content and photos you have provided us, whether directly or through your social media accounts, as well as messages you have sent and other data from the Grindr social media service.

Below is a data dictionary that we are providing for your convenience so you can understand the data returned to you. The data dictionary is a list of data file types contained within your data download and a description of the information included in those files. If a field is blank in your file, it means that we do not have that data for you, and nulls are expected for some fields. The data shown below is stored by Grindr's servers (rather than your mobile device). Also, please note that the data returned to you reflects the data we currently have stored pursuant to our Privacy Policy. Timestamps are in Coordinated Universal Time (UTC) unless otherwise indicated.

For additional information on how we use and share data, please refer to our Privacy Policy, or copy and paste this link: https://www.grindr.com/privacy-policy/.
